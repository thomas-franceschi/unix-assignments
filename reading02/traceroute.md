TLDR - traceroute
==========

Overview
--------

[Traceroute] is a command used to track the route a packet takes from the user's 
machine to its destination.

Examples
--------

- ** -4, -6** forces either ipv4 or ipv6 tracerouting:
    
    $ traceroute -4 www.google.com
